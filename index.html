Research & Innovation

Connector-Aware LLM Pretraining

The Problem: LLMs often struggle with long-range logical consistency due to "gradient starvation" on crucial connective words (e.g., "therefore", "however").

The Innovation: I engineered a "Connector Embedding Amplification" mechanism for Llama 3.2. By identifying 150+ discourse markers and boosting their signal during training, I forced the model to pay attention to the logic of the sentence, not just the content.

  • 10% theoretical acceleration in logic learning
  • Validated across 64k scientific documents
